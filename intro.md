# Commitments under Threat
## Online Appendix and replication code for:


>Basu, Karna and Jonathan Conning (2021) [Commitments under Threat: Contracting with Present-Biased Consumers facing Renegotiation Concerns](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1-tPu-6ZRqCZPznk5VTPu2-XQ-ACKarju/view?usp=sharing), manuscript.


Click on navigation links at left to replicate the paper's different figures, explore how contracts change with changing parameter values, or access online appendix materials. Python code for all of the figures and simulations can be found at [Contract.py](https://github.com/jhconning/commitments/blob/main/notebooks/Contract.py) and usage demonstrated in the notebooks.
